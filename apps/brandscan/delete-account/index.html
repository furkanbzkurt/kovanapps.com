Delete Your Data

You can request deletion of all data associated with your BrandScan usage. Since BrandScan uses anonymous authentication, no personal account exists — but we still store some data tied to your anonymous ID.

What data does BrandScan store?

BrandScan uses anonymous authentication — we do not collect your name, email, or phone number. The following data is associated with your anonymous user ID:

  1. Scan history — records of brands you have scanned (stored in our database)
  2. Subscription status — purchase records managed by RevenueCat
  3. Analytics data — anonymous usage statistics collected by Firebase Analytics
  4. Crash reports — diagnostic data collected by Firebase Crashlytics

Camera images are never stored. They are deleted immediately after AI analysis.

How to request data deletion

Send us an email and we will delete all data tied to your anonymous ID.

  1. Email us at info@kovanapps.com
  2. Use the subject: “Data Deletion Request – BrandScan”
  3. Include your anonymous user ID (you can find this in the app under Settings → About)
  4. We will process your request and confirm deletion within 72 hours

What happens when you request deletion?

  1. Your scan history is permanently deleted from our database
  2. Your anonymous user ID is deleted from Firebase Authentication
  3. Analytics and crash data are anonymized and cannot be linked back to you
  4. If you have an active subscription, it will not be automatically canceled — please cancel it through your app store settings before requesting deletion
Note: Since BrandScan uses anonymous authentication, once your data is deleted, it cannot be recovered. A new anonymous ID will be created if you continue using the app.
